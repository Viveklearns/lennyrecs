## 📚 Books

### Persuasion by Robert Cialdini
**Ada's top pick.** A breakdown of strategies for getting people to say yes; useful for marketers, founders, and product people.

- [View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/006124189X)
- ISBN: 006124189X

![Book Cover](https://books.google.com/books/content?id=_m-GDwAAQBAJ&printsec=frontcover&img=1&zoom=1)

### Designing Your Life by Bill Burnett & Dave Evans
From the Stanford D School. Applies design principles to career and life planning. Ada recommends it for people feeling stuck on career strategy.

- [View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/1101875321)
- ISBN: 1101875321

![Book Cover](https://books.google.com/books/content?id=XzqOCwAAQBAJ&printsec=frontcover&img=1&zoom=1)

### Make Time by Jake Knapp & John Zeratsky
Mentioned by Lenny as frequently recommended by other podcast guests. Contains "The Highlight" framework for picking the one thing to do each day.
